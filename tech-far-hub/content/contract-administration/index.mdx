When you’ve completed all the pre-award activities, from acquisition planning through evaluation and award, it’s time to focus on delivery. This is the Contract Administration phase of the acquisition lifecycle, and successful agile delivery requires continued participation and involvement from the government procurement and program teams. Use this section to learn more about Contract Administration & Audit services, how modifications work in agile contracts, subcontracting policies and procedures, guidance on quality assurance, and what to know about terminating agile contracts.
