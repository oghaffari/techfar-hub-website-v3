The pre-solicitation phase of the acquisition lifecycle is where planning, market research, and requirements development occur. In this section of the TechFAR Hub, you will find an overview of agile development to familiarize yourself with this exciting methodology, resources to support your acquisition planning activities, insights and questions to facilitate market research, and tools and guidance related to developing requirements.
