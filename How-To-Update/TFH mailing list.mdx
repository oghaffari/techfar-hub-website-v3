## Making changes

- To make changes to the administrators, go to https://identity.apps.pitc.gov/coi/#/.
- Enter the word 'Tech' in the search window
- Click on DL OMB TechFARHub
- Click on add personnel to membership to add and choose settings or check the box of the person you want to remove
